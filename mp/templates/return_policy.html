Return Policy

Thank you for shopping with us. We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Please review our return policy to ensure a smooth return process.

Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return, your item must meet the following criteria:

  • Must be returned within 30 days of receipt.
  • Must be in its original condition and packaging.
  • Must have a valid receipt or proof of purchase.

Return Process

To return an item, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team at supergs290@gmail.com to initiate a return.
  2. Provide your order number and the reason for the return.
  3. Once approved, you will receive a return authorization and instructions on how to send back the item.
  4. Pack the item securely and send it to the address provided in the return instructions.
  5. Upon receipt of the returned item, we will process your refund or exchange within 7-10 business days.

Non-Returnable Items

Some items are non-returnable, including:

  • Personalized or customized products
  • Perishable goods
  • Gift cards
